# Customer Support FAQ

## Account Management

### What are your business hours?

Our customer support team is available:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM EST
- Sunday: Closed

For urgent issues outside business hours, please email support@example.com and we'll respond as soon as possible.

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit our website homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email through the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile information

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new credentials

If you don't receive an email, check your spam folder or contact support.

### How do I change my email address?

To change your email address:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Account Settings → Profile
3. Click "Change Email Address"
4. Enter your new email
5. Verify the new email address through the confirmation link

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:
1. Go to Account Settings → Security
2. Scroll to "Delete Account" section
3. Click "Delete My Account"
4. Confirm by entering your password
5. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Note: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Yes, you can create multiple accounts using different email addresses. However, each account must have a unique email and cannot share subscription benefits unless you have a family or team plan.

## Billing & Payments

### How do I update my billing information?

To update billing information:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Billing → Payment Methods
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter your new card details
5. Click "Save Changes"

For invoices, go to Billing → Invoices to download past statements.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (for enterprise customers)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay

We currently do not accept cryptocurrency or prepaid cards.

### How long does it take to receive a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 2-3 days to appear in your account. If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, please contact support.

### Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. To request a refund:
1. Contact support within 30 days of purchase
2. Provide your order number
3. Explain the reason for refund (optional)
4. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

After 30 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to Account Settings → Subscriptions
2. Select the subscription you want to cancel
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for unused time.

### When will I be charged?

You'll be charged:
- Immediately upon initial subscription
- On the same date each billing cycle (monthly or annually)
- For any add-ons or upgrades immediately upon purchase

You'll receive an email receipt for each charge.

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

To change your plan:
1. Go to Account Settings → Subscriptions
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes! We offer:
- Student discount: 50% off with valid .edu email
- Annual plan discount: Save 20% when paying yearly
- Team plans: Discounts for 5+ users
- Non-profit discount: 30% off for registered non-profits

Contact sales@example.com for custom enterprise pricing.

## Contact & Support

### How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through:
- **Email**: support@example.com (Response within 24 hours)
- **Live Chat**: Available on our website during business hours
- **Phone**: 1-800-SUPPORT (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- **Help Center**: help.example.com for self-service articles

### What information should I include when contacting support?

Please include:
- Your account email address
- Order number (if applicable)
- Detailed description of the issue
- Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
- Steps you've already tried

This helps us resolve your issue faster.

### How long does it take to get a response?

Response times:
- Email: Within 24 hours (usually faster)
- Live Chat: Immediate during business hours
- Phone: Immediate during business hours
- Social Media: Within 4 hours

Premium and Enterprise customers receive priority support with faster response times.
